## Degree
Unit of <mark class="hltr-pink">angle</mark>: $1/360$ of a full rotation
$6^\circ$ = 6 degrees


## Arcminute
Unit of <mark class="hltr-pink">angle</mark>: $1/60$ of a degree
$4'$ = 4 arcmin


## Arcsecond
Unit of <mark class="hltr-pink">angle</mark>: 1/60 of an arcminute, $1/3600$ of a degree
$12''$ = 12 arcsec
